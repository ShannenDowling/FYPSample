About Us

Located on the north coast of Kerry, OceanView B&B is a family owned and run, bed and breakfast accomodation house. We cater to families of all ages and sizes, tourist groups and students alike. We are a just a short walk from Banna Beach, and only 10 minutes away from Kerry's largest town, Tralee. For directions please click here.

Our rooms availabe include, twin rooms, single rooms, double rooms and family rooms. For more details see our information page, where you will also find our room rates.

Some facilities and services we offer are laundry facilities, kitchen, dining room, games room, wifi service and television room. A sports ground and playground are also available across the road from our establishment. To see more click here.

Local attractions include the glorious banna beach, Ardfert cathedral, the beautiful seaside town of Ballyheigue and many more. You can view a complete and more detailed list, also on our information page.

Check in times are between 1pm and 3pm. Check out time is 12pm. We are checked by B&B Ireland and accept credit and debit cards as forms of payment, along with cash and cheques.

We operate in euro, as that is the official currency in Ireland. Our local weather is usually relativly pleasant but do expect rain if you come to our part of the world. We would advise checking the local weather forecast when planning your trip as the weather can sometimes be unpredicatble.

Unfortunately we do not accomodate for pets in our bed and breakfast. If a guest has a visual impairment and requires a guide dog, we would be more than willing to accomodate them, in any way we can, once notice is given, when booking a room. We also have a no smoking policy in our rooms but we do provide smoking facilities outdoors.
